1. who am i

Hey, I'm Raj. I am a Software Engineer with more than 5 years of professional experience. In my time as a developer, I've been able to work on a wide range of projects, enabling me to constantly learn new technologies while also pushing me to master core concepts of the developmental tools or languages I am adopting. I am passionate about building efficient, tailored applications focused on users and their needs. I get excited by the applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning to data and how those insights can fundamentally alter programs and companies.

I graduated from Washington University in St. Louis in 2013 with a Bachelors in Psychology and in 2014 with a Masters in Computer Science.

I currently live in San Francisco, CA and work remotely for Sparo, a biomedical tech start-up based in St. Louis, MO. As a software engineer on a small team, I've had to be self-reliant, creative, and strategic to learn and develop efficient, reliable software for people with respiratory afflictions like asthma and COPD.
